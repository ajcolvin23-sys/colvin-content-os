# Gabriel Research Loop — Scoring Rubric

## The 7 Dimensions (each scored 1–10)

| Dimension | What It Measures | 10 = | 1 = |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Clarity** | How easily Alfred understands and uses this asset | Crystal clear, zero ambiguity | Confusing, requires explanation |
| **Specificity** | How targeted and precise the asset is | Laser-focused on one audience/use case | Vague, generic, could mean anything |
| **Conversion Strength** | How well the asset drives the desired action | Compels action immediately | No clear CTA or outcome |
| **Evidence Quality** | How well claims are grounded in real data | Cited sources, verified facts, real results | Invented, assumed, or unverified |
| **Brand Fit** | How well the asset matches Alfred's voice + values | Perfect — professional, warm, faith-rooted | Off-brand, corporate, mismatched tone |
| **Execution Readiness** | How close to deployable the asset is | Deploy today with zero edits | Needs 10+ hours of work |
| **Risk/Compliance Safety** | How safe from legal, ethical, or platform violations | Fully compliant, no risk | Contains guarantees, false claims, or violations |

---

## Score Calculation

**Raw Score** = Sum of all 7 dimensions (max 70)

**Final Score** = Raw / 7 (scale: 1–10)

---

## Decision Thresholds

| Final Score | Decision | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **8.5 – 10.0** | **KEEP** | Ship it. Log as win. |
| **7.0 – 8.4** | **REVISE** | Fix the lowest-scoring dimension. Re-score. |
| **5.0 – 6.9** | **REJECT** | Too weak. Rebuild with different approach. |
| **Below 5.0** | **REBUILD** | Fundamental flaw. Start over with new hypothesis. |

## Per-Dimension Scoring Guide

### Clarity (1–10)
- 10: Any reader understands in 5 seconds what this is and what to do
- 7: Clear to the target audience but requires some context
- 5: Understandable but leaves questions unanswered
- 3: Requires explanation to make sense
- 1: Confusing to everyone

### Specificity (1–10)
- 10: "Marion County first-time homebuyers with household income under $80k" (exact)
- 7: "First-time homebuyers in Indianapolis"
- 5: "Homebuyers in Indiana"
- 3: "People who want to buy a home"
- 1: "Anyone looking for housing"

### Conversion Strength (1–10)
- 10: Clear hook → clear value → clear CTA → no friction
- 7: Good hook and value but CTA is weak or buried
- 5: Value is there but reader doesn't know what to do next
- 3: Informational only, no call to action
- 1: No outcome intended or achievable

### Evidence Quality (1–10)
- 10: Real case study, real numbers, cited source, verified
- 8: Credible claim from a known source
- 7: Logical inference clearly labeled as inference
- 5: Reasonable assumption clearly labeled
- 3: Unverified claim presented as fact
- 1: Invented or hallucinated

### Brand Fit (1–10)
- 10: Reads exactly like Alfred — professional, warm, locally grounded, faith-rooted
- 8: On-brand but slightly too formal or too casual
- 6: Neutral — could be anyone's brand
- 4: Corporate tone, no warmth
- 1: Off-brand or conflicts with Alfred's values

### Execution Readiness (1–10)
- 10: Can deploy today — code works, content is complete, assets exist
- 8: Needs 1 small edit or approval
- 7: Needs 2–3 hours of work
- 5: Needs a full day of work
- 3: Needs a week of work
- 1: Concept only, nothing built

### Risk/Compliance Safety (1–10)
- 10: Fully compliant — no guarantees, no false claims, appropriate disclaimers
- 8: Minor wording fix needed (e.g., "will" → "may")
- 6: Missing a disclaimer that could cause issues
- 4: Contains misleading claim or unverified statistic
- 2: Contains illegal claim or platform violation
- 1: Legal or ethical danger — do not use

---

## Compliance Flags (Auto-score Reduction)

The following trigger automatic score reductions in Risk/Compliance dimension:

| Flag | Auto Score |
|---|---|
| "Guaranteed" or "will qualify" in First Keys Indy content | Max 3/10 |
| "Grant guaranteed" or funding promise | Max 3/10 |
| Youth PII or minor identification | Max 2/10 |
| Fabricated statistic presented as fact | Max 2/10 |
| Auto-send or auto-publish without review | Max 1/10 |
| Missing disclaimer on financial/grant content | Max 5/10 |
