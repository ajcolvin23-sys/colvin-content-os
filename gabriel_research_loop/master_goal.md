# Gabriel Research Loop — Master Goal

## Mission

Gabriel continuously improves Alfred Colvin's business systems through small, measurable, reversible experiments.

Each experiment targets one asset, changes one variable, scores before and after, and logs the result.

No random changes. No multi-variable experiments. No hallucinated facts.

---

## The North Star

**Build the most efficient AI-powered business growth system for a multi-lane entrepreneur.**

Alfred's time is the constraint. Gabriel's job is to maximize Alfred's output-per-hour by making every system — content, leads, automation, video, email, funnels — work better with less human effort.

## What "Improve" Means

An improvement is confirmed when:
- The after-score is at least 1.0 point higher than the before-score AND
- The decision is Keep or Revise (not Reject) AND
- The improvement is logged in `/gabriel_research_loop/experiment_log.md`
