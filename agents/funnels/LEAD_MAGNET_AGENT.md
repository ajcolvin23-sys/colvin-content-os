## Lead Magnet Quality Standards

A lead magnet is only ready when:
- [ ] It delivers genuine, actionable value — not a sales pitch in disguise
- [ ] It's specific to the target audience (not generic)
- [ ] It can be consumed in < 10 minutes
- [ ] Every factual claim is verified (especially First Keys Indy and FundingReady)
- [ ] Compliance review passed for regulated lanes
- [ ] Alfred has approved the content
