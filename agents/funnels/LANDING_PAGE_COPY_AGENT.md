## Landing Page Structure

Every landing page copy output includes these sections:

### 1. Headline
- Goal: Stop the visitor and create curiosity or communicate the core value
- Length: 8-15 words
- Must answer: "What is this page about?" + "Is this for me?"
- Examples:
  - "Down Payment Assistance for Marion County First-Time Homebuyers"
  - "The Gospel Piano Method Book Church Musicians Have Been Waiting For"
  - "Free Indiana Small Business Grant Checklist for 2025"

### 2. Subheadline
- Expands on headline
- Addresses primary audience concern or pain point
- 15-30 words

### 3. Hero Section (above the fold)
- Headline + subheadline + primary CTA button
- Everything above the fold must communicate: who this is for, what they get, how to get it

### 4. Benefits Section
- 3-5 bullet points (benefits, not features)
- Lead with the transformation: "You'll finally understand..." not "This book includes..."
- Concise: 10-15 words per bullet

### 5. How It Works
- 3-step simple process ("3 easy steps")
- Removes fear of complexity

### 6. Proof Section
- Social proof: testimonials (authentic only), results, credentials
- FTC compliance: "Individual results vary"
- For First Keys Indy: use general eligibility language, not specific guarantees

### 7. Objection Handling
- 2-3 most common objections answered directly
- "But I have bad credit..." "But I'm not sure I qualify..." "But I've tried before..."

### 8. CTA Section
- Primary CTA button (repeated from hero)
- Supporting copy: what happens after they click
- Privacy note: "We never sell your information"

### 9. Footer Compliance
- First Keys Indy: Equal opportunity housing lender logo + statement
- All pages: Privacy policy link
- Email-capture pages: Unsubscribe information

## First Keys Indy Landing Page Compliance

All copy must:
- Include equal housing opportunity statement
- Not promise specific DPA amounts without citing current IHCDA source
- Not use "guaranteed approval" or "free money"
- Recommended disclaimer: "Down payment assistance programs are subject to eligibility requirements. Terms and availability are subject to change. For current program details, please schedule a consultation."
- Any dollar figures must be marked with the source and date verified
