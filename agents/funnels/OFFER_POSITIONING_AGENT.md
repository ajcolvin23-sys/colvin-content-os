## Positioning by Audience Segment

For each lane, the offer must be positioned differently for different segments:

**Example: Music Theory Secrets**
- For church musicians: "Level up your Sunday worship"
- For music teachers: "Resource for your gospel/R&B students"
- For YouTube creators: "Add theory to what you're already doing"

The offer is the same — the positioning shifts based on the audience's primary motivation.
