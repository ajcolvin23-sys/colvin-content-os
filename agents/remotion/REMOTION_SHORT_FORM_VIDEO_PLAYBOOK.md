# Remotion Short-Form Video Playbook — Colvin Content OS

Playbook for TikTok / Reels / YouTube Shorts. 30-60 second format. Hook-first, one idea, strong CTA.

---

## The 3-Second Rule

You have 3 seconds before a viewer swipes away. The hook must deliver in those 3 seconds:
- Visual: Something that makes them look
- Audio/Text: Something that makes them stay
- Promise: An implicit "if you watch, you'll get [value]"

---

## Short-Form Structure

### The 45-Second Gospel

```
0:00-0:03  — HOOK SCENE
  Visual: Bold text + striking visual
  Spoken: The hook statement
  Goal: Create curiosity / name the problem

0:03-0:12  — SETUP
  Visual: Context or contrast
  Spoken: "Here's what most people do / think / know..."
  Goal: Validate the hook, build tension

0:12-0:35  — VALUE DROP
  Visual: The teaching / demo / reveal
  Spoken: The actual value delivery
  Goal: Fulfill the hook's promise

0:35-0:42  — PROOF (optional for short videos)
  Visual: Quick social proof or credibility signal
  Spoken: "This is why X works / what I've seen / what clients say"

0:42-0:45  — CTA
  Visual: Logo + CTA text
  Spoken: One clear action
  Goal: Tell viewer what to do next
```

## Platform-Specific Rules

### TikTok
- Hook: Must work in 1.5 seconds at fast scroll speed
- Length: 30-60 seconds (sweet spot: 42-55 seconds)
- Captions: Required. Large. ALL CAPS or Title Case.
- Music: Background track adds energy. Must be licensed.
- Text overlay: Short phrases. 1-2 per scene max.
- Hashtags (in caption): 3-5 max. Mix trending + niche.

### Instagram Reels
- Same structure as TikTok
- Length: 15-90 seconds (sweet spot: 30-60)
- Cover frame matters for grid aesthetic
- Caption: Can be slightly longer than TikTok (Instagram users read more)

### YouTube Shorts
- Hook by 5 seconds is acceptable
- Can have slightly more narrative setup
- 60 seconds max for Shorts
- Description: 100-300 characters + relevant hashtags
- "Subscribe" is an acceptable CTA here

### Facebook Reels
- Older demographic — slightly slower pace OK
- Caption: More complete sentence text works
- Local content performs well (Indiana-specific references)

## Music Theory Secrets — Short Video Approach

Best-performing formats for this lane:

**Format 1: The Chord Reveal**
- Hook: Play a chord or run that sounds impressive
- Setup: "Most pianists can't name what they just heard"
- Value: Name the chord, explain the theory, show the fingering
- CTA: "Follow for more gospel theory"
- Duration: 30-45 seconds

**Format 2: The Comparison**
- Hook: Play the "basic" version vs the "gospel" version
- Setup: "Here's why these sound so different"
- Value: Explain the theory behind the upgrade
- CTA: "Get the full breakdown in my book"
- Duration: 45-60 seconds

**Format 3: The Step-by-Step Lesson**
- Hook: "3 chords that make you sound like a gospel pro"
- Value: Show each chord one by one
- CTA: "Save this for practice"
- Duration: 45-60 seconds

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Starting with "Hi, I'm..." — lose 50% of viewers
- Slow intro music for 5+ seconds before talking
- Too many ideas in one video (pick ONE)
- Weak CTA: "Let me know in the comments" with no specific prompt
- Captions that appear too fast to read
- Text that's too small on mobile
- More than 5 hashtags on TikTok (algorithm flags it)
