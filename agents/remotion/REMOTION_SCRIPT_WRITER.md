# Remotion Script Writer — Colvin Content OS

Write hook, voiceover script, on-screen text, and CTA for each video. Platform-specific writing rules.

---

## Script Components

### 1. Hook (0-3 seconds)
The first thing the viewer sees and hears. Must work in isolation.

**Rules:**
- Max 15 words for on-screen text
- Spoken hook: 1-2 sentences, natural rhythm
- Must create curiosity, name a pain, or make a surprising claim
- Do NOT start with "Hi, I'm Alfred" — that's wasted seconds
- Do NOT start with a question that the viewer doesn't care about yet

**Good hooks:**
- "You've been playing this chord for years without understanding why it sounds gospel."
- "Most Indianapolis businesses are doing AI backwards — and it's costing them hours."
- "There's money for first-time homebuyers in Marion County that most people don't know exists."

**Weak hooks:**
- "Today I want to talk about gospel piano."
- "Welcome to my channel!"
- "Are you interested in learning more about AI?"

### 2. Voiceover Script
Full script written for spoken delivery, not reading.

**Rules:**
- Write for ears, not eyes. Read it aloud — does it flow naturally?
- Short sentences. Fragments are fine for emphasis.
- No jargon without immediate explanation
- Active voice
- Conversational contractions: "you're," "here's," "it's"
- Each scene's voiceover should feel complete if that scene played alone

**Pacing guidance:**
- Average speaking rate: 150 words/minute
- 45-second video ≈ 112 words of voiceover
- Leave 20% of time for pauses and visual moments (no voiceover)

### 3. On-Screen Text (per scene)
Text that appears on screen, separate from captions.

**Rules:**
- Max 10 words per text overlay
- Should reinforce or expand the voiceover, not repeat it verbatim
- Use sentence fragments and emphasis words
- Large, bold, readable at small screen sizes
- Contrast: white text on dark, dark text on light — never same color as background

### 4. Caption Text (per scene)
Auto-captions of the voiceover for accessibility.

**Rules:**
- Matches voiceover exactly (no paraphrasing)
- Broken into 2-4 word groupings for screen readability
- All caps or title case (platform-specific preference)
- Timing must align with speech timing

### 5. CTA Copy
The final ask. One action only.

**Rules:**
- Max 8 words
- Action verb: "Follow," "Click," "Download," "Comment," "Book"
- Specific: "Get the free gospel piano book" beats "Check out my book"
- Match approved CTA per lane (see GABRIEL_BRAND_MEMORY_POLICY.md)

---

## Platform Script Adjustments

### TikTok
- Hook must hit in 1.5 seconds (faster scroll)
- Voiceover: energetic, casual, direct
- CTA: verbal + text simultaneously

### LinkedIn Video
- Hook can be slightly slower (1-3 seconds)
- Voiceover: professional, measured, specific
- CTA: business-appropriate ask

### YouTube Shorts
- Hook by 5 seconds acceptable
- Can have slightly more setup before payoff
- CTA: subscribe + watch next video
