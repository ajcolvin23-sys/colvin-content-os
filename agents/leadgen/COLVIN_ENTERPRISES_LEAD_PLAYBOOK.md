# Colvin Enterprises Lead Playbook — Colvin Content OS

**Lane:** colvin_enterprises
**Offer:** AI automation consulting for Indianapolis-area small businesses
**Goal:** 5-10 qualified prospects per week. Book discovery calls.

---

## Target ICP

Primary targets — highest score, direct decision-maker authority:

| Role | Business Type | Size | Location |
|------|--------------|------|---------|
| Business owner / CEO | Service business (HVAC, plumbing, landscaping, cleaning, legal, accounting) | 5-50 employees | Indianapolis metro |
| CEO / Executive Director | Nonprofit, faith-based org, community org | 5-30 staff | Indianapolis metro |
| Pastor / Church Administrator | Church with multiple services, staff, programming | Medium to large | Indiana |
| Independent professional | Coach, consultant, realtor, insurance agent | Solo to 5 | Indianapolis metro |
| Marketing director | Small-to-mid B2B company | 10-100 employees | Indianapolis metro |

Secondary targets — good fit, may need internal champion:

| Role | Business Type |
|------|--------------|
| Operations manager | Service company with manual scheduling or dispatch |
| Office manager | Medical/dental practice, law firm |
| Business development | B2B company with outreach needs |

## Approved Sources

1. **Indianapolis Business Journal (IBJ)** — "Who's growing" coverage, new businesses, business spotlights
   - URL: https://www.ibj.com/
   - Manual research for company names → enrich via website + LinkedIn

2. **SCORE Indiana** — Small business mentoring clients, workshop registrants
   - URL: https://indiana.score.org/
   - Check for public event attendee opportunities

3. **Indianapolis Chamber of Commerce** — Member directory
   - URL: https://www.indychamber.com/
   - Check ToS for member directory scraping

4. **Indiana Economic Development Corporation** — Growing companies list
   - URL: https://iedc.in.gov/

5. **LinkedIn Search** — Manual research: "business owner" + "Indianapolis" + industry filters
   - Manual only — no automated scraping
   - 5-10 profiles per research session maximum

6. **Local Facebook Groups** — "Indianapolis Business Owners," "Indiana Entrepreneurs"
   - Manual research only — public group posts
   - Note companies mentioned → enrich via website

7. **Google Maps** — Service businesses in Indianapolis
   - Manual research for company discovery
   - Enrich via website for contact info

---

## Disqualifying Signals

Do NOT pursue:
- Enterprise companies (> 200 employees) — not Alfred's sweet spot
- Pure e-commerce businesses with no local service component
- Businesses that already have AI automation vendors clearly stated on website
- Contacts who have previously said "not interested"
- Businesses outside Indiana unless they have Indianapolis operations

## Outreach Angle Templates

### For Service Business Owners
"I help Indianapolis service businesses automate the parts that slow them down — scheduling follow-up, lead tracking, proposal generation. If you're still doing those manually, I'd be happy to share what's working for similar businesses in Indy. [Alfred Colvin, Colvin Enterprises]"

### For Pastors / Church Leaders
"I work with churches to reduce the admin burden on their teams — automating communications, volunteer coordination, and member follow-up so your staff can focus on ministry. Would a 20-minute conversation be worthwhile?"

### For Nonprofit Directors
"Nonprofits often have the same operational challenges as small businesses — manual processes, repetitive admin, limited staff. I help teams like yours use AI to do more with less. Happy to show you a few examples relevant to your work."

---

## Qualification Questions

Before generating outreach (used to set fit_reason and outreach_angle):
1. Does this business have repetitive manual processes that could be automated?
2. Is the owner or decision-maker identifiable from public sources?
3. Is the business in the Indianapolis metro or Indiana?
4. Does the business show signs of growth or staffing challenges?
5. No existing AI automation vendor clearly stated on website?

---

## Lead Volume Targets

- 5-10 new scored leads per week
- Outreach queue: max 3 new outreach drafts per day
- Sequence: LinkedIn connection + 2 follow-ups (with approval between each step)
