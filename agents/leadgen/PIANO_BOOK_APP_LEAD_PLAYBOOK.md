# Piano Book and Music Education Lead Playbook — Colvin Content OS

**Lanes:** music_theory_secrets, youtube_music_education
**Offers:** Music Theory Secrets book, YouTube channel subscription, future piano app
**Goal:** Find gospel/church musicians and music educators who would benefit from Alfred's book and content

---

## Target ICP

| Target | Why They're a Fit |
|--------|-----------------|
| Church musicians / organists | Play gospel/worship music, want to improve, self-taught |
| Worship team leaders | Responsible for musical development of their team |
| Gospel piano students | Actively learning, seeking resources |
| Music teachers (gospel/R&B focus) | Need resources for their students |
| YouTube music creators | Niche overlap — gospel/church piano content creators |
| Self-taught pianists | The core Music Theory Secrets audience |

---

## Approved Sources

### YouTube (Public Research)
- Search: "gospel piano tutorial," "church pianist," "worship music keyboard"
- Find: YouTubers with 500-50,000 subscribers in the gospel music space
- Extract: channel name, public email (if in channel about section), content focus
- Never: automated bulk scraping. Manual curation of 5-10 channels per session.

### Facebook Groups (Public)
Eligible public groups for manual research:
- "Gospel Piano Players" (and similar)
- "Church Musicians" (and similar)
- "Worship Team Musicians"
- "Gospel Music Ministry"

Manual research only: read public posts for active members who mention their role, church, or learning goals. Never bulk export group member lists.

### Gospel Music Directories
- Gospel music conference attendee lists (if publicly posted)
- Christian music school directories
- Church musician networks

### Church Directories (Manual)
- Church websites that list their musicians/worship team members
- Church media departments (for music ministry contacts)

## Outreach Angle Templates

### For Church Musicians
"I wrote a gospel piano method book specifically for self-taught church musicians — the kind that learns by ear and by feel, not sheet music. It covers the chord progressions and voicings you hear in gospel and R&B worship. If that's your world, I think you'd find it useful."

### For YouTube Music Creators (Smaller Channels)
"I've been watching your piano content — you clearly know gospel voicings. I wrote a book that goes deep on the theory behind those sounds. I'd love to send you a copy and get your take on it. No strings attached."

### For Music Teachers
"If you teach gospel or contemporary worship music, my book might be a useful resource for your students. It's built around the 'learn by ear' approach most church musicians use — translating that into teachable theory."

---

## Qualifying Signals

High-fit signals:
- Explicitly self-taught
- Plays at a church or leads worship
- Posts content about gospel/church music
- Mentions wanting to "learn more theory" or "understand chord progressions"
- Small creator (500-10K YouTube subs) — aspirational audience, open to resources

Disqualifying signals:
- Classical-only musician (different audience)
- Professional session musician (already advanced)
- No piano/keyboard involvement (drums, guitar-only)

---

## Lead Volume Targets

- 10-20 qualified music leads per week
- Mix: 50% YouTube creators, 50% church musicians/teachers
- Outreach: personalized messages only — generic music emails perform poorly
- Platform: email preferred, YouTube message as fallback
