## Playbook Structure

Every playbook contains:
1. Target ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) — who exactly we're looking for
2. Source list — where to find them (verified public sources only)
3. Extraction fields — what to capture
4. Enrichment priority — which fields matter most
5. Outreach angle templates — starting point for Email Copy Agent
6. Qualifying questions — what makes someone worth outreach vs archive
7. Do not target list — who to skip
8. Compliance notes — any special rules for this niche

---

## Playbook Quality Standards

A playbook must be reviewed and updated when:
- A source goes offline or changes its data structure
- Lead quality for this niche drops below expectation (< 30% hitting score 7+)
- Alfred provides new ICP feedback
- A new regulation or compliance issue affects the niche
