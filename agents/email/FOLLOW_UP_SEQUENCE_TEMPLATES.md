# Follow-Up Sequence Templates — Colvin Content OS

Templates for all multi-touch sequences by lane. Each step requires Alfred's approval before sending.

## Colvin Enterprises 3-Touch Sequence

**Goal:** Book a discovery call or get a "yes, interested" response
**Total touches:** 3 (LinkedIn connection + 2 emails)
**Timeline:** ~14 business days

### Step 1: LinkedIn Connection Request (Day 0)
**Type:** linkedin_connection
**Character limit:** 300 characters
**Template:**
```
Hi [First Name], I help Indianapolis service businesses automate the manual work that slows their teams down. [Specific company reference if available]. Worth connecting? — Alfred Colvin, Colvin Enterprises
```

### Step 2: Email (Day 3-5 after connection accepted)
**Type:** email
**Subject line:** "AI automation for [Company Name]?"
**Template:**
```
Hi [First Name],

Thanks for connecting. I noticed [specific detail about their business from research].

I work with Indianapolis-area businesses to build AI workflows that handle [specific pain point relevant to them] — typically saving 10-15 hours per week without adding headcount.

If that sounds relevant, I'd love to show you what's working for similar businesses in Indy. 20 minutes of your time?

[Book a call link or "just reply to this email"]

Alfred Colvin
Colvin Enterprises | Indianapolis, IN

To stop receiving emails, reply and let me know.
```

### Step 3: Follow-Up Email (Day 8-10 after Step 2)
**Type:** email_followup
**Subject line:** "Still relevant? [Company Name] + AI automation"
**Template:**
```
Hi [First Name],

Just wanted to make sure my last email didn't get buried.

If now's not the right time, no worries — happy to reconnect when it makes sense. If you're open to a quick conversation, I'm at [email] or [phone if provided].

Alfred

To stop receiving emails, just reply and let me know.
```

## First Keys Indy 5-Touch Welcome Sequence (Opt-in Only)

**Goal:** Educate, build trust, convert to consultation booking
**Triggered by:** Lead magnet download or website opt-in
**Total touches:** 5 emails over 14 days

### Email 1: Welcome (Immediate)
**Subject:** "Your Marion County Homebuyer Guide is here"
Welcome email, guide delivery, set expectations for sequence.
HUD disclaimer included: Program terms subject to change.

### Email 2: Education (Day 3)
**Subject:** "What is Down Payment Assistance (and do you qualify)?"
Explain DPA simply. Dispel myths. Invite to learn more.
No specific amounts without current IHCDA source.

### Email 3: Story (Day 6)
**Subject:** "How a Marion County family went from renting to owning"
Anonymous success story. Warmth and relatability. CTA: schedule call.

### Email 4: Myth-Busting (Day 9)
**Subject:** "3 things that stop people from applying (and how to get past them)"
Address common objections: credit score, income, process complexity.

### Email 5: CTA (Day 14)
**Subject:** "Ready to check your eligibility?"
Direct CTA to schedule a consultation. Final email in sequence.

## Music Theory Secrets 5-Email Book Launch Sequence

**Goal:** Convert engaged music audience to book buyers
**Triggered by:** Lead magnet (free piano lesson download)
**Total touches:** 5 emails over 10 days

### Email 1: Value Delivery (Day 0)
**Subject:** "Your free gospel piano lesson"
Deliver the lead magnet. Introduce Alfred briefly. Set expectations.

### Email 2: Teaching (Day 2)
**Subject:** "The chord your music teacher didn't cover"
Educational email. Teach one gospel chord concept. Reference the book as "more of this."

### Email 3: Story (Day 4)
**Subject:** "Why I wrote Music Theory Secrets"
Alfred's story. Why this book exists. Emotional connection.

### Email 4: Proof (Day 7)
**Subject:** "What church musicians are saying about the book"
Social proof (authentic testimonials Alfred has collected). Book link.

### Email 5: Offer (Day 10)
**Subject:** "Your copy of Music Theory Secrets"
Direct book offer. Price. What's inside. CTA: Buy now.
FTC note: Include "individual results vary" near testimonial references.
