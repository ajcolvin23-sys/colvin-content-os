# Platform Style Guide — Colvin Content OS

Rules for every platform Alfred publishes on. Alfred's voice: professional + warm + educational + faith-rooted.

---

## LinkedIn

**Purpose:** Professional authority, B2B leads, AI consulting visibility
**Audience:** Business owners, executives, professionals in Indianapolis and Indiana
**Tone:** Thoughtful, specific, credible. Peer-to-peer, not advisor-to-client.
**What works:** Real examples, specific numbers, personal stories with business lesson, contrarian takes

### Format Rules
- Hook line: 125 characters max — must stand alone
- Body: 150-300 words optimal (max 3,000 chars)
- Line breaks: Every 1-3 sentences for readability
- Paragraphs: Max 3-4 sentences
- Lists: 3-7 items max, each short
- Hashtags: 3-5, placed at end
- Emojis: Optional, used sparingly (max 1 per post — Alfred's brand is not emoji-heavy)
- CTA: Question, opinion invite, or offer (not "like and share")

### Alfred's LinkedIn Voice
"I saved a plumbing company 12 hours a week by automating their dispatch follow-up. Here's the exact workflow I built and what I'd do differently the second time."
(Specific → Here's how → Lesson = Alfred's LinkedIn voice)

## TikTok

**Purpose:** Top-of-funnel, new audience discovery, Music Theory Secrets primary growth channel
**Audience:** 18-45, gospel/church musicians, self-taught pianists, faith community
**Tone:** Engaging teacher. Revelatory. "You need to know this."

### Format Rules
- Hook: Must hit in first 3 seconds (first 1-2 sentences of script)
- Length: 30-60 seconds (video via Remotion)
- Caption: 100-150 characters
- Hashtags: 3-5 total
- Text-on-screen: Short phrases, not sentences. One point at a time.
- CTA: One CTA only — "follow for more" or "link in bio"

---

## Instagram

**Purpose:** Visual brand building, community, Music Theory Secrets and First Keys Indy
**Audience:** Visual-first consumers, Marion County homebuyers, gospel music community
**Tone:** Warm, visual, story-driven

### Format Rules
- Captions: 150-300 words (optimal), up to 2,200 (max)
- Hashtags: 5-15 for posts, fewer for Reels
- Stories: Text overlays max 3 lines. Quick, punchy.
- Carousels: 5-10 slides. First slide is the hook. Last slide is CTA.
- Always include image direction/description for Remotion or designer

---

## Facebook

**Purpose:** Community building, Marion County audience (First Keys Indy), gospel music community
**Audience:** 25-55, local Indiana community, faith-based, homebuyers
**Tone:** Community helper, conversational, trust-building

### Format Rules
- Post length: 100-250 words (organic reach often better at medium length)
- Hashtags: 0-3 (Facebook doesn't drive discovery via hashtags like Instagram)
- Video: Remotion video performs well — native upload preferred
- CTA: Direct and action-oriented: "Comment below if you'd like more info"
- First Keys Indy: All posts must include HUD-compliant language

---

## YouTube

**Purpose:** Authority building, top-of-funnel for Music Theory Secrets and Colvin Enterprises
**Audience:** Music learners, faith-based content consumers, small business professionals
**Tone:** Educational authority. "Here's exactly how to do this."

### Format Rules (Shorts)
- Length: 60 seconds max
- Script: Hook → one clear teaching point → CTA
- Title: Question format or "how to" or number hook
- Thumbnail (separate from Remotion): High contrast, large text, one face or clear visual

### Format Rules (Long-form — script only, not Remotion)
- Length: 8-15 minutes for music tutorials, 5-10 for business content
- Structure: Hook (60s) → Setup/problem → Teaching → Demo → Recap → CTA
- Description: SEO-optimized, includes timestamps, links

---

## Email

**Purpose:** Nurture, conversion, relationship
**Tone:** One-on-one conversation. Alfred writes to one person, not a list.
**Format:** Short paragraphs, no bullet-point newsletters (unless specifically appropriate), plain text or minimal styling

### Format Rules
- Subject lines: 40-60 characters optimal
- Preview text: 100 characters max
- Body: 100-300 words for nurture emails, up to 500 for educational
- One CTA per email
- Always: unsubscribe link, physical address
