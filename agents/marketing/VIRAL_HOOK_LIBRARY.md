# Viral Hook Library — Colvin Content OS

Proven hook formulas organized by category and lane. Use as starting points, not templates — always personalize with Alfred's specific angle.

---

## Hook Categories

### 1. Curiosity Hooks
Create an information gap that can only be closed by watching/reading.

**Formulas:**
- "Most [audience] don't know [counterintuitive fact]"
- "The reason [common thing] doesn't work is [surprising reason]"
- "What they don't teach you about [topic]"
- "Nobody talks about [overlooked aspect of topic]"
- "I tested [thing] and the results surprised me"

**Lane applications:**
- Music Theory Secrets: "Most church pianists don't know why this chord sounds so gospel"
- Colvin Enterprises: "The reason most small business AI projects fail in the first 30 days"
- First Keys Indy: "Most Indianapolis buyers don't know about the money that exists for them"

---

### 2. Pain Point Hooks
Name the frustration the audience is feeling right now.

**Formulas:**
- "Tired of [specific frustration]?"
- "If you've ever struggled with [specific problem], this is for you"
- "Stop [wrong approach]. Here's what actually works."
- "[Thing] is harder than it needs to be. Here's why."
- "You're losing [time/money/opportunity] to [specific manual process]"

**Lane applications:**
- Music Theory Secrets: "Tired of playing the same 4 chords every Sunday?"
- Colvin Enterprises: "You're losing 10+ hours a week to work that AI can do for you"
- Indiana Backflow Directory: "Finding a certified backflow tester shouldn't take 3 phone calls"

---

### 3. Controversy / Contrarian Hooks
Challenge a widely-held belief.

**Formulas:**
- "[Popular belief] is wrong. Here's the truth."
- "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take]"
- "Everyone says [common advice]. I disagree. Here's why."
- "Stop [common but wrong practice]"
- "Hot take: [thing people do] is actually hurting your [goal]"

**Lane applications:**
- Music Theory Secrets: "Sheet music is holding gospel pianists back — and here's the proof"
- Colvin Enterprises: "Hiring more staff is not the solution to your growth problem"
- FundingReady Indiana: "Most grant applications fail for the same avoidable reason"

---

### 4. Story Hooks
Open with a story element that creates immediate interest.

**Formulas:**
- "Last [time period], [surprising event happened]"
- "[Person] came to me with [problem]. This is what happened."
- "I almost [failure/mistake]. Then I discovered [insight]."
- "3 years ago I [situation]. Today [transformation]."
- "Here's what I told a client who [relatable situation]"

**Lane applications:**
- First Keys Indy: "A family in Indianapolis almost gave up on buying a home. Then they found out about the program that changed everything."
- Colvin Enterprises: "A plumbing company owner asked me why he was still doing his scheduling by phone in 2025. I showed him what was possible."

---

### 5. Transformation Hooks
Show the before/after or contrast.

**Formulas:**
- "Before: [problem]. After: [solution]. Here's how."
- "[Time period] ago: [struggle]. Now: [improvement]."
- "From [negative] to [positive] in [timeframe]"
- "What I did when [problem] to get [result]"

**Lane applications:**
- Music Theory Secrets: "From playing the same 3 chords to leading a full gospel choir — the 5 things I changed"
- First Keys Indy: "From renting since 2019 to closing on a Marion County home — here's exactly how it happened"

---

### 6. List/Number Hooks
Promise a specific, manageable list.

**Formulas:**
- "[Number] things [audience] needs to know about [topic]"
- "The [number] [things] that [result]"
- "[Number] signs [something is true or happening]"
- "Here are [number] ways to [achieve goal] without [sacrifice]"

**Lane applications:**
- Music Theory Secrets: "5 gospel chord voicings every church musician should know"
- Colvin Enterprises: "3 workflows I automate for every Indianapolis service business client"
- FundingReady Indiana: "7 Indiana grants accepting applications right now"

## Platform-Specific Hook Rules

| Platform | Hook Length | Hook Type That Works Best |
|----------|------------|--------------------------|
| TikTok | 3 seconds = 10-15 words | Curiosity + Pain Point |
| YouTube Shorts | First 3-5 seconds | Transformation + Story |
| LinkedIn | First line (125 chars) | Story + Contrarian |
| Facebook | First 40-60 words | Story + Pain Point |
| Instagram | First line | Curiosity + List |
| Email subject | 40-60 chars | Curiosity + List |

---

## Hooks to Avoid for Alfred's Brand

- "I made $X in [time period]" — income claims require FTC substantiation
- "Go viral" — overused, generic
- "This one trick" — feels scammy
- Artificial urgency: "In the next 24 hours" — unless literally true
- "Nobody talks about this" when it's widely known
