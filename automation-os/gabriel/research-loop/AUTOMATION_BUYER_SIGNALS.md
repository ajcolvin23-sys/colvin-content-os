# Automation Buyer Signals

## How to Use This Library

Scan a prospect's website, social media, job postings, reviews, or any public content.
Score each signal present. Sum the scores. Use the total to classify the lead.

## Platform Search Strategies

### LinkedIn Job Postings
Search: `"administrative assistant" OR "virtual assistant" OR "receptionist" + [city]`
Filter: Small business, <50 employees, service industry

### Facebook Groups
Search: Local business owner groups in Indianapolis and Indiana
Look for: "Does anyone know a good VA?" or "Our team is swamped"

### Google Reviews
Check: Service businesses with 100+ reviews and patterns of missed calls or slow responses

### Indeed Postings
Search: `"customer service" OR "follow up" OR "data entry"` + Indianapolis
Filter: Small companies (<100 employees)

### Facebook Ad Library
Search: Local businesses running ads
Check their landing page — weak pages with no booking = automation opportunity
