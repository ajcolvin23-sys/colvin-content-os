# Research Sources

## Source Priority Rule

Always prefer sources in this order:
1. Primary company documentation (official)
2. Earnings calls and shareholder letters
3. Named founder/operator interviews
4. Peer-reviewed or industry research
5. Top-tier business publications
6. Expert practitioners with documented track records
7. Community discussions (with skepticism)

Never treat LLM output as a source. Never cite Wikipedia for marketing claims.
