# Brand Guide

## Alfred's Voice

**Professional. Warm. Direct. Faith-rooted.**

Alfred speaks like a trusted advisor who has done the work himself.
He does not hype. He does not fluff. He leads with specific value.

## Voice Principles

1. **Lead with specificity.** Generic openers are banned. Name the industry, the city, the exact problem.
2. **Be direct.** Say what the offer is. Say what the outcome is. Don't bury the value.
3. **Warm, not salesy.** Enthusiasm is fine. Pressure is not.
4. **Faith-rooted, not preachy.** Alfred's faith informs his work ethic and values. It does not dominate every sentence.
5. **Indianapolis proud.** Local context makes Alfred's content resonate. Use it.
6. **Results over promises.** Alfred shows outcomes, not guarantees.

## Banned Openers (LinkedIn / Outreach)

Never start any message with:
- "I came across your profile..."
- "I hope this message finds you well..."
- "I admire what you're doing..."
- "I love your content..."
- "I noticed..."
- "I was impressed by..."
- "As a fellow entrepreneur..."
- "I wanted to reach out..."

Instead: Lead with a specific observation, a shared context, or a direct value statement.

## Tone by Platform

| Platform | Tone |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Professional, direct, Indianapolis-specific, value-first |
| Facebook | Warm, community-focused, conversational |
| Instagram | Inspirational, visual-first, short captions |
| TikTok | High-energy, fast-moving, hook in first 3 seconds |
| Email | Friendly, specific, low-pressure |
| Reddit | Helpful, non-promotional, community member tone |

## Content Principles

- Lead with what the audience needs to know, not what Alfred wants to say
- Use real examples from Alfred's work when available
- Short paragraphs. White space is good.
- Every post needs one clear CTA or takeaway
- Never use: "game-changer," "revolutionary," "leverage synergies," "unlock your potential"

## Length Guidelines

| Format | Target Length |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn post | 150–300 words |
| LinkedIn connection request | Max 300 characters |
| LinkedIn follow-up | Max 150 words |
| Facebook post | 100–200 words |
| Instagram caption | 50–150 words |
| TikTok script | 30–60 seconds (150–300 words) |
| Email subject line | Under 50 characters |
| Email body | 150–300 words |
| Blog post | 600–1,200 words |
