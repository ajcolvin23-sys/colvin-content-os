# ICP Library

## colvin_enterprises — AI Consulting ICP

**Primary:** Small business owner, 5–50 employees, Indianapolis metro
- Pain: Repetitive manual workflows eating time and money
- Trigger: Just hired someone to do data entry / scheduling / reporting manually
- Language they use: "I need to automate this" / "I'm drowning in admin"
- Decision authority: Owner makes the call, no committee
- Budget signal: $2K–$15K project range
- Where to find: LinkedIn (Indianapolis, operations/founder title), local chamber events

**Secondary:** Marketing director at Indianapolis professional services firm
- Pain: Content production is slow, agency is expensive
- Trigger: Just lost a marketing contractor or agency
- Budget signal: $1K–$5K/month retainer

## indiana_backflow — Directory ICP

**Primary:** Property manager, 50+ units, Indiana
- Pain: Tracking backflow testing compliance across multiple properties
- Trigger: City compliance notice or upcoming inspection
- Language: "certified tester" / "backflow prevention" / "compliance due"
- Where to find: LinkedIn (property manager, Indiana), Facebook landlord groups

**Secondary:** Facility director at Indiana commercial building
- Pain: Same as above but single large building

## music_theory_secrets — Gospel Piano ICP

**Primary:** Church musician, self-taught, wants to read music and play by ear simultaneously
- Pain: Can't read sheet music well, can't explain what they're playing
- Trigger: New role at church, worship leader asked them to lead
- Language: "gospel chords" / "how do I play like that" / "learn gospel piano"
- Where to find: Facebook gospel music groups, YouTube comments, Reddit r/churchmusic

**Secondary:** Music teacher wanting to add gospel/contemporary skills
- Pain: Classical training only, congregation wants contemporary sound

## first_keys_indy — Homebuyer ICP

**Primary:** Marion County renter, 25–45, household income $40K–$80K
- Pain: Doesn't know how to start the homebuying process, scared of rejection
- Trigger: Lease renewal, rent increase, first baby
- Language: "how do I buy a house" / "down payment help" / "first time buyer"
- Where to find: Facebook first-time homebuyer groups, Reddit r/indianapolis
- Compliance: Never say "you will qualify" — always direct to HUD-approved lender

## funding_ready_indiana — Grants ICP

**Primary:** Indiana small business owner, 1–20 employees, minority or women-owned
- Pain: Doesn't know what grants exist or how to apply
- Trigger: Business is 1–3 years old, looking to grow
- Language: "small business grant" / "Indiana funding" / "SBA loan"
- Where to find: LinkedIn (Indiana founder/owner), Reddit r/Indiana, Facebook business groups
- Compliance: No guarantee of award, no legal advice

## girls_got_game — Community ICP

**Primary:** Parent of a girl in youth sports, Indianapolis area
- Pain: Wants more than athletics — wants leadership development
- Trigger: Looking for summer program or after-school activity
- Language: Community, leadership, girls, sports
- Compliance: Youth-safe, no PII for minors — Katrina review required

## glory_engine — Faith Media ICP

**Primary:** Faith-based reader, 18–40, enjoys Christian/biblical storytelling in creative formats
- Pain: Most faith content is boring or preachy — wants something engaging and artistic
- Trigger: Discovering a new faith-based comic, YouTube Short, or Instagram reel
- Language: Biblical, inspiring, creative, faith, purpose
- Where to find: Instagram faith accounts, TikTok Christian creators, YouTube Shorts
