# Idea Browser Growth — Examples

## Example 1: Opportunity Scoring

**Idea:** Build a "Grant Readiness Checklist" lead magnet for FundingReady Indiana

**ICP:** Indiana small business owners and nonprofits who have heard about grants but don't know if they qualify

**Pain identified:** "I want grants but I don't know where to start or if I'm eligible"

**Offer score breakdown:**
- Clarity: 8 — simple concept, clear outcome
- Specificity: 7 — Indiana-specific, small business focused
- Conversion strength: 7 — checklist format is high-completion
- Evidence quality: 6 — based on known grant landscape; not yet validated with Alfred's audience
- Brand fit: 9 — matches Alfred's community-helper brand
- Execution readiness: 8 — checklist can be built in one session
- Risk/compliance: 7 — no funding guarantee; must include "contact an advisor" disclaimer

**Total:** 52/70 | **Final score:** 7.4 — REVISE before launching

**What to improve:** Evidence quality. Run a 5-question survey with FundingReady Indiana email list to validate the specific pain before investing in the full lead magnet.

**Recommended validation path:** Survey first. Build if 60%+ of respondents confirm they want this.

## Example 2: Build vs. No-Build Decision

**Idea:** Create a TikTok channel for Indiana Backflow Directory to explain backflow testing

**ICP:** Homeowners in Indiana who need backflow certification

**Assessment:**
- Backflow testing is a low-interest, low-frequency need (homeowners don't wake up thinking about backflow)
- TikTok requires consistent content for months to build an audience
- Better path: SEO + Google Business Profile (higher intent traffic)

**Decision:** No-build on TikTok for Backflow Directory. Invest in SEO instead. Log as rejected in decisions.md.
