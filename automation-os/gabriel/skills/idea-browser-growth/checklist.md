# Idea Research Pre-Delivery Checklist

- [ ] Idea stated in one clear sentence
- [ ] ICP identified: who, what pain, what trigger
- [ ] Minimum 3 real sources found via Firecrawl (not from memory)
- [ ] All 5 scoring dimensions rated with evidence
- [ ] Total score calculated
- [ ] Best landing page angle identified
- [ ] Recommendation: build/test/skip with reasoning
- [ ] No hallucinated statistics or invented market data
- [ ] Passed to qa-publish-guard before delivery
