# Content Engine — Examples

## Example 1: Strong LinkedIn Post

**Lane:** colvin_enterprises
**Platform:** LinkedIn
**Goal:** Build authority + lead magnet awareness

"3 signs your Indianapolis business is ready for AI automation:
→ You're hiring someone to manage spreadsheets
→ You're missing calls during busy hours
→ Your team does the same task 20 times a week

Each of these is a system problem. Systems can be automated. DM me "AUDIT" and I'll show you what that looks like for your business."

**QA Notes:** Specific geography, specific signals, soft CTA, no hype language, no guaranteed outcomes. ✅

## Example 2: TikTok Hook

**Lane:** music_theory_secrets
**Platform:** TikTok
**Goal:** Gospel piano education, drive to book

"Wait — you've been playing this chord wrong your whole life. [PAUSE] This is the chord most gospel pianists play: [demo wrong chord] Here's what the pros actually play: [demo right chord] Follow for the full gospel chord system."

## Example 4: Banned Opener (What NOT to Write)

"I hope this message finds you well. As someone with deep experience in AI consulting, I wanted to reach out to connect about potential synergies between our organizations."

**Why it fails:** Contains banned opener ("I hope this message finds you well"), hype language ("synergies"), and is generic with no specific pain reference.
