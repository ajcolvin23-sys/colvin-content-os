# Elite Company Analysis — Examples

## Example 1: Good Run — Shopify's Distribution Lens

**Company:** Shopify
**Lens:** Distribution Strategy
**Source:** Shopify Partner Blog (Primary) + Stratechery analysis (High)

**Finding:** Shopify's growth flywheel is driven by their Partner ecosystem — agencies, developers, and app builders who bring merchants to the platform. Shopify invests heavily in making partners successful (partner revenue share, Shopify Academy) because each successful partner brings 10–50 merchants.

**Evidence Level:** 3 — documented in Shopify's official Partner documentation and validated by Stratechery's independent analysis.

**Scale Gap:** Shopify has millions of partners. Alfred has zero. However, the PRINCIPLE scales: build one successful case study client who becomes a referral source, not one transaction.

**Alfred's Application:**
For Colvin Enterprises — after delivering a successful AI automation project, immediately ask: "Who do you know who runs a similar business?" Build a formal referral incentive program (not just asking — a structured program like Shopify's).

**Extracted Workflow:**
1. After project completion, deliver a documented case study (with client permission)
2. Send a "Partner Offer" — if they refer one client, they get 30 days of free automation support
3. Give referred client a case study of the referrer's results as social proof
4. Track referrals in Supabase as a separate lead source

**Proposed Skill Update:** Add referral workflow to `content-engine` or create a new `referral-engine` workflow.
