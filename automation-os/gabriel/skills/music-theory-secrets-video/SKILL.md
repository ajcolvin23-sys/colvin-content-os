## Core Teaching Concept

Music theory is not random. It is patterns.

**Default micro-lesson for every video:**
The Nashville Number System applied to the 1–5–6–4 progression.

```
Numbers:  1  →  5  →  6  →  4
In C:     C  →  G  →  Am →  F
In G:     G  →  D  →  Em →  C
In D:     D  →  A  →  Bm →  G
In F:     F  →  C  →  Dm →  Bb
```

**Rule:** Every Music Theory Secrets video teaches something usable in this session. The micro-lesson IS the hook.
