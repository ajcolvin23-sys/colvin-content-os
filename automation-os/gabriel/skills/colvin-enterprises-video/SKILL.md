## Compliance Language — MANDATORY

### Always Use
- "May save time"
- "Can help reduce repetitive work"
- "Identify automation opportunities"
- "Find what can be systemized"
- "Improve follow-up"
- "Reduce manual work"
- "Many businesses find they can free up hours"
- "The audit may reveal..."

### Never Use
- "Guaranteed revenue"
- "Guaranteed hours saved"
- "AI will replace your team"
- "Guaranteed growth"
- "Fully automate your entire business instantly"
- "Guaranteed leads"
- "Guaranteed ROI"

## Core Pain Points (use in task-leak + lost-revenue scenes)

In order of emotional impact:
1. Missed follow-ups — leads going cold because nobody followed up
2. Manual emails — drafting the same message over and over
3. Slow lead response — hours or days to respond to new inquiries
4. Copy/paste work — data moving between systems by hand
5. CRM updates — team forgetting to log calls, meetings, notes
6. Appointment reminders — sending manually, missing no-shows
7. Leads falling through cracks — no system to catch dropped balls
8. Repeating the same customer answers — FAQ work eating support time
9. Business owner stuck in operations — can't grow because they're doing everything
10. Too much admin work — time going to tasks that don't move the business forward
