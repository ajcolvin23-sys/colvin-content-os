# Website CRO — Examples

## Example 1: Headline Improvement

**Lane:** colvin_enterprises
**Problem:** Low click-through on the "Book a Call" CTA
**Diagnosis:** Unclear offer — visitors don't know what they're getting on the call

**Current:** "Book a Free Consultation"
**Improved:** "Book Your Free AI Automation Audit — 30 Minutes, No Pitch"

**Hypothesis:** If we clarify what the call delivers ("AI Automation Audit") and remove the fear of being sold to ("No Pitch"), booking rate will improve by ≥15%.

**Risk level:** Medium (active traffic page)
**Recommended action:** A/B test via experiment-platform-workflow

## Example 2: Social Proof Repositioning

**Lane:** first_keys_indy
**Problem:** Low form fill rate on the homepage
**Diagnosis:** Missing proof closest to the CTA

**Current layout:** Hero → Programs → About Alfred → CTA form
**Improved layout:** Hero → Programs → Testimonial/success story → CTA form

**Hypothesis:** Moving a specific Marion County buyer success story directly above the CTA form reduces the last objection before submitting.

**Recommended action:** Direct replacement (low traffic, new arrangement, no copy change)

## Example 3: Pricing Presentation

**Lane:** colvin_enterprises
**Problem:** No pricing page; Alfred gets "how much?" questions on every call
**Diagnosis:** Missing pricing section = friction before the conversation starts

**Improvement:** Add a simple 3-tier pricing anchor section:
- Tier 1: AI Workflow Audit — $497
- Tier 2: Implementation Package — from $2,997
- Tier 3: Monthly Retainer — from $1,497/month

**Note:** Do NOT list actual scope. Use "from" pricing to anchor while staying flexible.
**Risk level:** Medium — new page section, no existing CTA replaced
**Recommended action:** Add to page, monitor with Humblytics for scroll depth and CTA click rate
