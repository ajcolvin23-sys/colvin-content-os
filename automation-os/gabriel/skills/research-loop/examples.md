# Research Loop (Meta) — Examples

## Example: 5-Session Research Program

**Topic:** How do top AI consulting agencies price their services?
**Goal:** Build Alfred's pricing framework for Colvin Enterprises

**Sessions:**
1. Session 1 — Top earner analysis: Solo AI operator pricing (Level 3, named public pricing)
2. Session 2 — Elite company analysis: Salesforce's tiered pricing model principles (Level 3)
3. Session 3 — Market research: AI agency pricing benchmark from Clutch.co report (Level 3)
4. Session 4 — Top earner analysis: Productized service operator pricing (Level 3, documented)
5. Session 5 — Conversion research: How pricing anchoring affects AI service sales (Level 2)

**Cross-session patterns:**
- Pattern 1: Tiered pricing (audit → implementation → retainer) appears in sessions 1, 2, 4 → Level 4
- Pattern 2: Audit product is the conversion gateway in sessions 1 and 4 → Level 3

**Final recommendation:** Alfred should offer a 3-tier structure: $497 Audit → $2,997 Implementation → $1,497/month Retainer. Audit is the front door.
