# Skill: Outreach Drafting

## Core Rule

DRAFT ONLY. Never send. Every message saved to `outreach_drafts` with `status = 'pending_review'`.

## Quality Standard for Each Draft

Before adding to review queue, every draft must pass:
1. Is it under the character/word limit for its platform?
2. Does it mention something specific about the recipient?
3. Does it avoid generic openers? ("I hope this finds you well" = instant fail)
4. Does it deliver value or open a relevant conversation (NOT pitch immediately)?
5. Is it free of compliance violations?
6. Does it sound like Alfred?

## LinkedIn Connection Request Template

**Limit:** 300 characters (hard limit — LinkedIn will reject longer)

**Formula:** [Observation about them] + [Brief credibility signal] + [Low-pressure ask]

```
Example:
"Saw your post on AI in operations — spot on. I help Indianapolis businesses automate their workflows without losing the human touch. Would love to connect."
[188 chars]
```

**Never use:**
- "I noticed we share X connection"
- "I came across your profile"
- "I'm reaching out because"
- Emojis in connection requests (looks automated)

## LinkedIn Follow-Up Template (after connection accepted)

**Limit:** 150 words

**Formula:** [Thank for connecting] + [Deliver value first] + [Soft question or next step]

```
Example:
"Thanks for connecting, [Name].

I put together a quick breakdown of the 3 AI workflows that are saving Indianapolis service businesses the most time right now — happy to share it if useful.

Also curious — what does your current ops/workflow setup look like? Are you using any AI tools yet or still evaluating?"
```

**Never:**
- Lead with a pitch
- Use "just wanted to check in"
- Start with "I"

## Email Outreach Template

**Subject formula:** [Pain point] + [Result] or [Curiosity gap]
- "How Indy businesses are saving 10 hrs/week with AI"
- "The grant most Indiana small businesses miss"

**Body formula:**
1. Open with their situation (not yours) — 1 sentence
2. Validate the challenge — 1 sentence
3. Brief credibility — 2 sentences
4. Low-pressure CTA — 1 sentence

## Personalization Checklist

Pull these before writing:
- [ ] Their current title and company
- [ ] What their company actually does (not just the industry)
- [ ] Any recent news (new hire, funding, event, content they posted)
- [ ] Why they specifically fit Alfred's offer (not just their industry)
- [ ] Which of Alfred's lanes serves them best
