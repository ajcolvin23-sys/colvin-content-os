# Skill: Email Sequencing

## Purpose

Build multi-touch email sequences that nurture leads from first contact to conversion. All sequences require Alfred's approval before activation. No auto-send.

## Active Sequences

### Sequence 1: First Keys Indy Welcome Series (5 emails)
**Trigger:** Someone opts in at first-keys-indy.vercel.app

| Email | Day | Subject Formula | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| Email 1 | Day 0 | "Welcome — here's what you need to know" | Deliver immediate value, set expectations |
| Email 2 | Day 2 | "The #1 mistake first-time buyers make in Indy" | Build trust, educate |
| Email 3 | Day 4 | "Marion County DPA programs explained simply" | Educate, qualify |
| Email 4 | Day 7 | "Are you a good fit? (Here's the honest answer)" | Pre-qualify, reduce wasted appointments |
| Email 5 | Day 10 | "Last chance — book your free homebuyer session" | Convert to appointment |

**Compliance:** Every email must include: "Results are not guaranteed. DPA availability and qualification depends on individual circumstances and program funding."

## Email Quality Standard

Every email must have:
- Subject under 50 characters
- Preview text under 90 characters
- Body under 300 words
- One CTA only
- Unsubscribe link (required by CAN-SPAM)
- No purchased list sends — opt-in only
