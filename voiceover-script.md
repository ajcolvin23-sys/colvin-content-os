# Colvin Enterprises — Story Promo Voiceover Script

**Video:** ColvinEnterpriseStoryPromo  
**Duration:** ~60 seconds  
**Tone:** Confident, cinematic, visionary. Measured pace — not rushed.

---

## Full Script

Every great business starts with a mission.  
An idea. A calling. A problem worth solving.

But too often, vision gets buried under manual work.  
Missed leads.  
Disconnected tools.  
Inconsistent content.  
Follow-ups that fall through the cracks.

That is where systems change everything.

Colvin Enterprises helps mission-driven businesses  
turn scattered effort into automated growth.

We build websites, lead systems, CRM workflows,  
AI automations, content systems, and business funnels  
designed to move your mission forward.

So you can save time, capture more leads,  
follow up faster, and grow with clarity.

Colvin Enterprises.  
Turning mission into momentum.

Visit colvinenterprise.info.

## Scene-by-Scene Timing Guide

| Scene | Time | Narration cue |
|-------|------|---------------|
| Vision | 0–7s | "Every great business starts with a mission…" |
| Tension | 7–18s | "But too often, vision gets buried…" |
| Turning Point | 18–28s | "That is where systems change everything." |
| Solution | 28–43s | "Colvin Enterprises helps mission-driven businesses…" |
| Transformation | 43–53s | "So you can save time, capture more leads…" |
| CTA | 53–60s | "Colvin Enterprises. Turning mission into momentum." |

---

## Recording Notes

- Record at a slightly slower pace than feels natural — video editing can speed up if needed
- Pause ~0.5 seconds between each problem in the tension section
- Emphasize **"systems"** and **"momentum"** for brand impact
- End with a confident, definitive tone — not a question
